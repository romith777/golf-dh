Draw mechanics

Three reward tiers with automatic prize pool distribution.

Prize pool split

You do not enter extra money manually for the draw. A fixed portion of every active subscription is automatically added to the monthly prize pool.

  • 5-number match: 40% and jackpot rollover if unclaimed
  • 4-number match: 35% split equally across winners
  • 3-number match: 25% split equally across winners
